Our Services

  • Incident Response

    Creating and implementing plans for responding effectively to cybersecurity issues. Involving a coordinated approach to contain, eradicate, and recover from a security breach while minimizing the impact

  • Managed Security Services

    Outsourcing daily cybersecurity activities to a third party vendor. This comprises continuous monitoring, threat detection, incident response, and security infrastructure management

  • Security Consulting

    Providing experienced advice and help for creating, executing, and improving cybersecurity plans. Security consultants identify risks, offer solutions, and help integrate security measures with corporate objectives

  • Penetration Testing

    Conducting controlled and authorized simulated cyber attacks to assess the security posture of a system. Ethical hackers attempt to exploit vulnerabilities to identify weaknesses and improve defenses
